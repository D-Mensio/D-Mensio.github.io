Projects

Horbo

Explore a procedurally generated labyrinth with infinite floors of increasing size, but beware of its inhabitants


Horbo is a 3D 1st person maze exploration game made for the Scream Jam 2021.


I decided to explore and implemented boids/flocking alghoritms (simulating the behaviour of birds) for the enemies.

As a setting, I decided to go with a procedurally generated maze, and set up loops patrolled by the enemies.

I also had lots of fun experimenting with an eerie setting, adjusting visuals, music and sfx!

Ashto

A relaxing abstract puzzle game for Android


Ashto is a 2D puzzle game in which you build paths to connect sources and destinations.


I wanted to work on a longer project (~2-3 months) to gain a better understanding of the work that goes in a game after the prototype phase, and focus not only on the mechanics, but also on the level design and presentation.

It was also interesting designing a game around mobile screens and controls.

Galaxy Raiders

Simple space invaders-like game (supported on both desktop and mobile devices)


This little game was made to test Html5 game development, with a particular focus on mobile devices.

I also chose to use Godot, to experiment developing with an engine different from what I am used to.

Island64

Explore and colonize a 64x64px procedurally generated island


Island64 is a 2D puzzle game made for the LowRezJam 2021, with the goal of making a game with a resolution of 64x64 pixels.

While the gameplay and mechanic are not quite developed enough, I had the opportunity to experiment with scripted camera movements, and a dynamic tiles system, switching sprites depending both on zoom level and neighbouring tiles.

Also my first venture in procedural content generation.

Everto

A short puzzle game with one handed controls.


Everto is a 2D puzzle game in which you have to figure out the correct input, made for the Mini Jam 76: Radiation, with a focus on gamepad controls and Unity's Input System.

Redo

A short platformer in which levels get easier when failing, and harder when succeeding.


Redo is a platformer made for the Wowie Jam 3.0, and also the first game I made.
